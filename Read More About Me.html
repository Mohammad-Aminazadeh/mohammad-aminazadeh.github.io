About Me

Full Autobiography

Before University

I was born in January 18, 1998. I earned a diploma in Mathematics with a grade point average of 18.26 out of 20 ranking among top students in high school of "Isfahan University of Technology". After taking "The National University Entrance Exam" (also known as "Konkur") and ranking 1,760 out of 165,000 Math and Physics examiners, I chose to pursue my bachelor's at the University of Isfahan.

My Bachelor's Journey

I studied chemical engineering for the first two years of my bachelor's degree, but I showed little interest in it. Even as a young child, I had a strong passion for "Problem Solving" and developed an interest in coding. These were the routes that led me to my major change in computer engineering, which was regarded as a challenging major requiring a wide range of "Problem Solving" and "Critical Thinking" skills. Due to my strong enthusiasm, my grades significantly increased as soon as I began studying my new major .

Other Interests and Hobbies

In addition to my academic interests, I consider myself a professional equity investor and am very interested in financial markets in order to find solutions to problems that others may find challenging or impossible to solve. I really enjoy using machine learning to explore knowledge in other academic disciplines.
I am also a level 3 swim coach and a level 2 lifeguard. I used to swim professionally. However, currently my concentration is on my academic goals and I just exercise to stay healthy.
