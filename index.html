About Me

I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from University of Isfahan (UI), Iran, in 2021. The first two years of my bachelor's, I studied Chemical Engineering. I changed major to Computer Science and graduated as a top rank student among other students in that department with a GPA of 3.84 (Last 3 years studying Computer Science).

I was honored to defend my Final Thesis under supervision of Dr.Noorbehbahani in the field of Big Data Analytics and Machine Learning. I am also interested in doing personal projects and in this regard I have done a web scraping project using Selenium, extracting financial data of 26,600 public companies around 10 countries over a period of 10 years and used them for further personal analysis.

I have published two research papers (54 Citations) in collaboration with my supervisor in journal of "Education and Information Technologies" and IEEE International Conference on Computer and Knowledge Engineering (ICCKE).
