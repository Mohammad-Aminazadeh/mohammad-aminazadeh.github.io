About Me

I obtained my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Isfahan (UI), Iran, in 2021. I studied Chemical Engineering for the first two years of my bachelor's. I switched my major to Computer Science and graduated as one of the top-ranking students among other students in that department with a GPA of 3.84 (In my last 3 years studying Computer Science).

I was honored to defend my Final Thesis under the supervision of Dr.Noorbehbahani in the area of Big Data Analytics and Machine Learning. I am also interested in working on personal projects, and in this regard, I have done a web scraping project using Selenium, extracting financial data from 26,600 public companies over a 10-year span in 10 different countries. I then used this data for other purposes.

Together with my supervisor, I co-authored two research papers that were published in the IEEE International Conference on Computer and Knowledge Engineering (ICCKE) and the journal "Education and Information Technologies" and received 54 citations.

Projects

  • City Traffic Data Analytics (Time Series Analysis and Visualization - Time Series Clustering)
    • I worked on a real-world project as my final thesis. In Isfahan, traffic sensors collected data for 37 months from 71 intersections, yielding 13 million lines of text-based traffic records. The data was cleaned, aggregated, and properly stored in order to be prepared for further time series and clustering analysis. In order to identify similar or dissimilar locations in the city for more effective traffic management, the main process of this project was to divide city intersections based on the similarity of their traffic flows. My secondary goal was to use time series visualization to examine daily variations in traffic flow at individual intersections and identify patterns. Results obtained from this project led to the publication of my second research paper.

      • Main Tools and Libraries Used: TSLearn - Pandas - Numpy - Matplotlib - Seaborn - Tableau Software

  • Financial Data Extraction and Aggregation (Web Scraping with Selenium - Data Aggregation and Cleaning - Statistical Analysis)
    • In this project, I used Selenium to extract massive amounts of data from the web. As a hobby and part of my personal projects, I extracted all possible financial features (reaching 380 features) from 26,600 public corporations listed on stock exchanges in 10 different countries. The data was cleaned and used for statistical analysis to gain financial insights for more thoughtful investing.
